What is a Travel Blog?

A Blog is a website that is updated regularly in the form of posts. These posts can be stories and/or snippets of information, reviews, photographs, images, podcasts and other types of content that can be uploaded online. A Travel Blog is simply a blog where the entire focus is on the world of travel.

Who is a Travel Blogger?
