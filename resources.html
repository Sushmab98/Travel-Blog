TRAVEL RESOURCES

TRAVEL INSURANCES

Prepare for the worst and make sure to buy travel insurance before you leave. Although, you should note that you can also buy insurance after departure if needed. Find more information on different travel insurances available below.

SafetyWing

SafetyWing offers insurance designed especially for nomads. It was founded by nomads who know what location independent people need from their travel insurance. They have an automatic monthly payment which is great if you’re traveling long term.

World Nomads

World Nomads offers simple and flexible travel insurance for travelers with diverse and changing travel plans. You can buy and claim it anywhere in the world and keep traveling without having to worry about your insurance. World Nomads insurance gives you wide coverage and covers the loss and damage of your electronics.

TRAVEL ESSENTIALS

Here are some travel resources we recommend when it comes to visas and Internet abroad.

Travel Visas

If you think you might need a visa for your upcoming destination – check iVisa. You can order an online visa if you need one.

Booking.com

Book your hotel, hostel, apartment and more for your next adventure. Booking.com has great deals so you’ll find good prices. Browse their options based on your destination or by property type.

GetYourGuide

Transform your trips into unforgettable experiences with GetYourGuide. They will make your journey unique and stress-free. Plan your trip and book your tickets without any hassle. Booking experiences and tours beforehand allows you to just enjoy the experience once you’re at your destination.
